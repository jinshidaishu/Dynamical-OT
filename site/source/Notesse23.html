A.1 Proximal Operator

Given the proximal operator \begin {align} u^* = \text {prox}_{g}(u^n) = \underset {u}{\mathrm {argmin}} \ g(u) + \frac {1}{2}\|u-u^n\|^2, \end {align}

we have that this is equivalent to \begin {align} & 0 \in u^* - u^n + \partial g(u^*) \\ \Leftrightarrow \ & u^n \in (I + \partial g) u^* \\ \Leftrightarrow \ & u^* = (I + \partial g)^{-1} u^n \end {align}

In other words, \(\text {prox}_g(\cdot ) = (I + \partial g)^{-1}(\cdot )\).
